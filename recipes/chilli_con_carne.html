Chilli con carne

How to make!

Chilli con carne is a great meal for a cold day. It's easy to make and can be made in large batches and frozen for later.

Ingredients

  • Beef Mince
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Chilli
  • Tomatoes
  • Red kidney beans
  • Beef stock
  • Tomato puree
  • Ground cumin
  • Ground coriander
  • Paprika
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Bay leaf
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Dark chocolate
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Chop Vegetables
  2. Fry Vegetables
  3. Add Mince
  4. Add Spices
  5. Add Tomatoes
  6. Add Stock
  7. Add Beans
  8. Add Chocolate
  9. Simmer for 1 hour
  10. Enjoy!
