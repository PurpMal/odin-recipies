Peanut Butter Cookies

How to make!

These cookies are very easy to make and are very tasty!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees C.
  2. Combine ingredients and mix until smooth.
  3. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking tray.
  4. Bake for 6-8 minutes.
